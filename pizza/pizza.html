Welcome to my pizza dough page

I will give you a short and simple recipe to make the best dough!

First the indigrients

  1. Water 600 ml
  2. Flour type 405 1kg
  3. Starter 0.5g
  4. Salt 40g
  5. lots of time and muscle power

The Holy Grail of manuals

  1. Start mixing water, 600g flour and salt
  2. Add starter
  3. Let it rest for 20m
  4. Add 200g of flour
  5. Mix and let it rest 20m
  6. prepare a clean tablet and pour the reminaing flour on it
  7. pour the dough on top and knete it again for atleast 10m
