How to make a tasty burger

What u need is an understanding of the intricate layering of a burger. Otherwise you wont be able to use the full potential of your indigrients.

Beware this burger is badly layred and will fall apart as you bite into itgit

Lets start with a list of potential upgrades to your burger

It's a given that you have made a plain well grilled patty and baked your brioche bun! Now a short list what u need!

  • Tomatos, San Marzano
  • fresh washed lettuce
  • selfmade mayonese
  • Ketchup and/or mustard
  • a block of matured cheddar or a chunck of Epoisse
  • finely chopped pickles
  • caramellised onions, also chopped into small 5mm pieces

Now all the magic is the way you stack it!

  1. Toast the inside of your buns, it has to be crispy
  2. Mayo on the inside of the bottom bun and ketchup and/or on the top bun
  3. now sprinkle the finely chopped pickles ontop of the bottom bun
  4. add the patty on top, followed by a layer of cheese
  5. if u took the cheddar, its time to melt it with a torch! stay professional! otherwise u can also take heated pan turn it upside down and place it on top of the patty
  6. now add the salad and garnish the tomato on top
  7. finally add the onions to the now sticky bottom side of your top bun
  8. take a step back and enjoy
